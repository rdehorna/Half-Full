Privacy

Don't worry -- Half Full operates in full compliance with HIPAA's Security Rule. This means that we vow to to protect the personal health information you share with us! Two glorious white tigers or lions or bears will be guarding your information in a database housed behind a secured firewalls (we have integrated our technologies into Amazon Web Services), and we hope to keep improving the options of this type of long-term monitoring platform to better help you! Many more applications and analyses will soon become available.
